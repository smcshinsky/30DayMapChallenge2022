## Day 14: Hexagons
Provo Urban Deer Control Program. Deer living among the population present both safety issues and property damage concerns for residents. A sample survey counted over 500 deer in the foothills of Provo over a span of three months. From 2013-2017 the City had over 1,000 deer-related calls to Provo Police. Vehicle-deer collisions in Provo endanger human life, and kill approximately 150 deer each year. The GIS Team was engaged to produce maps that might assist the Administration in their search for a well-focused solution. The resulting Urban Deer Control Program was approved by the City and DWR in 2016
